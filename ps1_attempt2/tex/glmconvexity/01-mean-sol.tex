\begin{answer}
Noting that $0 = \frac{\partial}{\partial \eta} \int p(y;\eta) dy = \int \frac{\partial}{\partial \eta} p(y;\eta)dy = \int p(y;\eta) [y - a'(\eta)] dy$ we get that $E(Y|\eta) = \int p(y;\eta)y dy = \int p(y;\eta) a'(\eta)dy = a'(\eta)$
\end{answer}
